📚 First — What is BBA? (Very Simple Explanation)
Start Here
BBA = Bachelor of Business Administration
Think of it like this:

Imagine you want to run a business one day — a shop, a company, or even your own startup. BBA is the course that teaches you HOW to run that business.

You will learn things like:
  • How to manage people (HR — Human Resources)
  • How to sell products (Marketing)
  • How to handle money (Finance)
  • How to run a company's daily work (Operations)
  • How businesses work in the real world (Business Law, Economics)

Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)  |  Maths needed? No ❌  |  Based on: Your 12th marks (no entrance exam in most Kerala colleges)
🌟 Why BBA is the RIGHT choice for you:
You passed Plus 2 Commerce → BBA directly continues from where your 12th left off. Same subjects you already know (Business Studies, Economics, Accounts) — but now deeper and more practical. Zero maths stress. 100% relevant to real-world jobs.

🎯 Your Main Question — "Will BBA be enough? Or do I need to waste time on extra courses after?"
Great question. Here is the honest answer:
BBA alone gives you a degree. But BBA + one good add-on skill = a very strong career. The good news? These add-ons are NOT 6-month waste-of-time courses. They are done DURING BBA itself (no extra time after). Most Kerala colleges offer them as part of the course already.

⚖️ Kerala vs Mangalore — Simple Comparison
🌴 Stay in Kerala (Kannur)
  • ✅ Fees as low as ₹10,000–₹50,000 for 3 years
  • ✅ Live at home — zero extra living cost
  • ✅ E-Grantz scholarship — can make it FREE
  • ✅ Familiar language, family support
  • ✅ Good for govt job preparation (PSC, Bank PO)
  • ❌ Smaller placement network than Mangalore
  • ❌ Less exposure to corporate environment

Best if: Budget is a concern, or she wants to prepare for government jobs.
🏙️ Go to Mangalore
  • ✅ Better college quality (especially St. Aloysius)
  • ✅ More corporate placements & industry exposure
  • ✅ Independence — builds confidence
  • ✅ Only 3 hours from home — can visit easily
  • ✅ Strong alumni network in banking/finance
  • ❌ Higher cost: ~₹1L–2L per year total
  • ❌ Kerala scholarships (E-Grantz) won't work here

Best if: Family can manage ₹1–1.5L/year and she wants better career exposure.
