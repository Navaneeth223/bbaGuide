# University Admission Meeting Checklist (BCA)

## Objective

My goal is to collect all important information about the BCA program and determine whether the university is the right choice for my future studies and career.

---

# Introduction

Use this introduction when meeting the admission counselor:

> Hello Sir/Madam.
>
> My name is Navaneeth. I am from Kerala. I am interested in joining the BCA program. I would like to know about the course structure, specializations, placements, internships, hostel facilities, fees, and scholarship opportunities.
>
> I am also interested in AI and Full Stack Development, so I would like to understand which specialization would be best for my career goals.

---

# Communication Support

If you do not understand something:

> Sorry, English is not my first language. Could you please explain slowly?

If you need more details:

> Could you please explain that in a little more detail?

If you need time to think:

> Thank you. I will note that down.

---

# Final Questions Before Leaving

* What is the admission deadline?
* How many seats are available?
* What is the admission process?
* What documents are required?
* When do classes start?
* Is there an orientation program?
* Who should I contact if I have further questions?

---

# Final Goal

Before leaving the campus, make sure you know:

* Total cost of study
* Hostel cost
* Placement opportunities
* Internship opportunities
* Specializations available
* Scholarship options
* Course quality
* Faculty quality
* Career opportunities after graduation

If all of these questions are answered, the visit will be successful and you will be able to make an informed admission decision.
